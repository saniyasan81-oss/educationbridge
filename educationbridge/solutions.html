Actionable Solutions

Proven strategies to close the education gap — and how you can help

Digital Learning Hubs

Solar-powered tablets and offline educational content bring schools to remote villages. Partner with local NGOs to deploy low-cost devices preloaded with curriculum.

Teacher Training & Support

Online certification programs and mentorship for unqualified teachers. Mobile coaching apps help instructors improve literacy and numeracy instruction.

Scholarship & Supply Drives

Community-funded scholarships cover fees, uniforms, and books. School-in-a-box kits provide basic supplies for 40 students.

Radio & TV Education

Interactive radio instruction (IRI) reaches millions in low-connectivity areas. Broadcast lessons in local languages at accessible times.
